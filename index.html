About Us

Kenokip Tech is a technology-driven brand committed to designing, implementing, and maintaining electrical and ICT systems, robotics, automation, and digital solutions. Our mission is to empower communities, students, and businesses with practical, sustainable technology solutions.

Founded in Marumbasi Village, Kericho County, Kenya, we combine innovation, skill, and modern technology to solve everyday challenges. Our team focuses on building projects that are educational, cost-effective, and environmentally responsible.

Our Services

Electrical & Electronics

Design, installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of residential, commercial, and industrial electrical systems. We also offer solar power solutions and electronics prototyping.

ICT & Networking

Setup of computer networks, server management, network security, and ICT training for students and businesses. We provide hands-on solutions for digital transformation.

Robotics & Automation

Introductory to advanced robotics projects including programmable robots, Arduino & Raspberry Pi automation, and small-scale industrial automation solutions.

Web & Branding

Professional website development, digital branding, social media content creation, and portfolio design. We focus on boosting visibility and professionalism for businesses and individuals.
