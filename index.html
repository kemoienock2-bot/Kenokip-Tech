About Us

Kenokip Tech is a technology-driven brand committed to advancing innovation in electrical engineering, ICT, robotics, and digital systems. We focus on building reliable, affordable, and scalable solutions tailored to local and global needs.

Our Vision

To become a leading technology and innovation hub that empowers individuals, institutions, and communities through smart, sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality electrical and ICT services
  • Promote robotics and automation skills for future-ready learning
  • Support digital transformation through web and branding solutions
  • Inspire innovation through practical technology

Core Values

  • Innovation with purpose
  • Technical excellence
  • Integrity and reliability
  • Community empowerment
  • Continuous learning

Our Services

Electrical & Electronics

Design, installation, troubleshooting, and maintenance of electrical systems for residential, institutional, and small commercial setups.

ICT & Networking

Computer systems setup, networking, basic cybersecurity practices, and hands-on ICT training for individuals and institutions.

Robotics & Automation

Introductory robotics and automation projects designed to build foundational skills in programming and problem-solving.

Web & Branding

Modern, responsive websites and digital branding solutions that help individuals and businesses establish a strong online presence.

Projects & Portfolio

Our work includes electrical installations, ICT training initiatives, robotics demonstrations, and website development projects for individuals and small businesses.
